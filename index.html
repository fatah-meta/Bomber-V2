Rules for the Game

You have to use your Brain!. In order to get all the boxes not having BOMBS in it. More over you are given 10 flags which you can use to mark the position which you think can have bomb inside it. On each click in the cell the points inside the cell will be reflected, based on the bombs contained in neighbouring cell. For side cells the selection will be a square of nearby cells(left, right, and rest cells in-front of the three), and for all other cell it will be rectangle of six cells(up, down, left, right, northwest, northeast, southwest, southeast).

Play with your intelligence.
